Resolution Action Guidelines

Follow these strict protocols when encountering different transaction risk levels to ensure user security and compliance.

Safe Transactions (Green)

What to Do

  • Allow the transaction to proceed normally.
  • Log the transaction details in the system history.

What NOT to Do

  • Do not contact the user for verification.
  • Do not apply any temporary holds on the account.

Warning Transactions (Yellow)

What to Do

  • Temporarily hold the transaction in pending state.
  • Call the user immediately or trigger an OTP to their registered mobile number.
  • Investigate location mismatch or sudden high transaction amounts.

What NOT to Do

  • Do NOT approve the transaction without explicit user consent.
  • Do not instantly assume fraud and panic the user; remain professional.

Danger Transactions (Red)

What to Do

  • Instantly block the transaction and freeze the ATM card/account.
  • Generate a Suspicious Activity Report (SAR) for the security team.
  • Notify the user formally about the security breach attempt and require them to visit a branch or reset credentials.

What NOT to Do

  • Under strict compliance, DO NOT process the funds.
  • Do not remove the block until full 100-point identity verification is completed.
